About Me

Fernanda Lozoya

Hello! My name is Fernanda Lozoya, and I am passionate about BMWs, Formula 1, and guitars. My love for cars, especially BMWs, stems from their engineering excellence and sleek designs. I enjoy staying up-to-date with motorsport events, particularly Formula 1, which keeps me on the edge of my seat every race weekend!

Aside from cars, I have a deep connection to music. Playing the guitar is not just a hobby but a way to express myself creatively. Whether it’s classic rock or modern tunes, my guitar is always by my side.

With roots in both Mexico and the United States, my heritage plays a significant role in shaping who I am. The blend of cultures gives me a unique perspective and fuels my ambition to explore diverse opportunities.
